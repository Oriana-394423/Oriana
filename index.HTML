Oriana's Volleyball Tips

Improve your skills and become a better volleyball player!

How to Set

  • Keep your hands above your forehead with thumbs together.
  • Use your fingertips to push the ball, not your palms.
  • Step under the ball to position yourself correctly.
  • Focus on accuracy rather than power.
  • Practice both forearm and overhead sets.
  • Communicate with your hitters before setting.

How to Spike

  • Approach with three steps and jump explosively.
  • Reach high and snap your wrist for power.

  • Practice timing with your setter.
  • Keep your eyes on the ball at all times.
  • Land safely with knees bent to prevent injury.

How to Serve

  • Stand behind the end line and toss the ball in front of you.
  • Focus on accuracy first, then power.
  • Experiment with float and topspin serves.
  • Keep your body balanced and swing smoothly.
  • Practice serving to different zones on the court.

How to Pass (Bump)

  • Keep your arms straight and hands together.
  • Angle your platform toward the target.
  • Step toward the ball with your opposite foot.
  • Focus on controlling the ball, not hitting it hard.
  • Communicate with teammates on the court.

Defense Tips

  • Always stay low with knees bent.
  • Keep your eyes on the ball at all times.
  • Move quickly to anticipate the hitter.
  • Practice digging both forearm and overhead shots.
  • Communicate and call out plays with teammates.

General Training Tips

  • Warm up and stretch before every practice.
  • Work on footwork, agility, and reaction speed.
  • Improve your strength with core and leg exercises.
  • Watch professional matches to learn strategies.
  • Stay hydrated and maintain good nutrition.
