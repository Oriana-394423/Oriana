Oriana's Volleyball Tips

Learn how to set, spike, serve, and become a better volleyball player!

Setting Spiking Serving Skills

Setting Tips

Hand Position

Keep your fingers spread, use your fingertips, and form a triangle with your thumbs and index fingers.

Footwork

Move your feet quickly and stay under the ball to maintain control and balance.

Soft Touch

Practice gentle releases to ensure the ball goes exactly where your hitter wants it.

Spiking Tips

Approach

Use a 3- or 4-step approach and jump explosively to reach maximum height.

Arm Swing

Swing your hitting arm back and forward quickly, snapping your wrist at contact.

Placement

Aim for open spots on the opponent's court rather than always hitting with power.

Serving Tips

Consistency

Practice overhand and underhand serves regularly to build accuracy and power.

Targeting

Aim for weaker passers on the other team or corners to make your serve more effective.

Follow-Through

Use a smooth follow-through motion to keep your serves accurate and strong.

Essential Volleyball Skills

Communication

Always talk to your teammates during the game to avoid collisions and improve teamwork.

Defense

Stay low, anticipate attacks, and practice digging and blocking to protect your court.

Conditioning

Work on endurance, agility, and leg strength to move quickly and jump higher during matches.
